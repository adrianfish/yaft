YAFT Pebble Trail

The pebble trail helps you to keep track of your location within YAFT's screens. If you clicked on a forum, then clicked 'Add Discussion' you would see something like this ...

... near the top of the tool area, just underneath the tool options. 'Forums' and 'My Forum' are both clickable links which will take you to the home forums page and the 'My Forum' page respectively.
